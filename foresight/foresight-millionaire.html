HOW MANY TEAMS?

HOW IT WORKS

  • PHASE 1 25 revision questions. All teams answer every question. 25 seconds to discuss within your group, then teams raise their answer cards (A/B/C/D) on the host's call.
  • SCORING Correct = bank the prize for that question. Wrong = nothing (no penalty).
  • LIFELINES Each team has 2 lifelines for the whole game: 50:50 and Ask Another Team.
  • PHASE 2 Every team enters the Millionaire Finale — 10 hard questions. All surviving teams answer each question; any team that answers incorrectly is knocked out and drops to the sidelines. Survive to bank ever-bigger prizes.
  • WINNER The last team standing — or, if several survive all 10, the team with the largest total bank.
