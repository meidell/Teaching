Mouna Chaari

Deputy Academic Director & Doctoral Supervisor · PhD

Dr. Mouna Chaari holds a PhD in Management from Université Paris Dauphine, specialising in education, research and innovation. She serves as Deputy Academic Director and Doctoral Supervisor at Business School Lausanne (BSL). Before joining BSL she was a Programme Manager at the Agence Nationale de la Recherche in France, focusing on technology transfer and programme impact, and later established the Career & Alumni Centre at South Mediterranean University.
