About this course

What it's about

This course equips you with the skills to manage complex AI projects. It covers advanced project-management methodologies, the AI-specific challenges that make these projects different, resource allocation, risk management, and the tools for planning and delivering AI systems that actually reach production.

Built on the official Cengage-aligned curriculum for E1410, personalised with real-world cases from the instructor's practice.

Prerequisites

Recommended but not mandatory: a basic knowledge of AI concepts and tools. Everything else is built up from Session 1.

What you'll be able to do

By the end of the course you will be able to:

  • Understand the principles of project management in AI-specific contexts.
  • Apply advanced project-management techniques to AI projects.
  • Manage resources and mitigate the risks tied to AI deployments.
  • Develop effective timelines and budgets for AI projects.
  • Evaluate AI project outcomes and ensure successful delivery.

Each session pairs a live class with an interactive lesson, a self-check quiz, and a running-project workbook — so the theory is practised, not just heard.

The course · session by session
Assessment · how the grade is built

50% · Mid-term — quiz exam

A 60-question multiple-choice exam on the UMEF LMS, covering Sessions 1–4. It tests the fundamental concepts: the nature of AI projects, planning, resources and risk.

The self-check quizzes in each session here are the best rehearsal — same style, instant explanations.

50% · Final — written case study

The recommended route builds all term: develop your running project — the one you fill in each session's workbook — into an in-depth written case study. (The topic-analysis and country-research options remain open if you prefer.)

Each session's workbook seeds one section of it; the final asks you to expand each into researched, critical analysis.

📄 Compile my project →

Assessed on critical thinking, research quality and practical application. Your workbook is the backbone — the grade is for the depth you add.
