The course · session by session
Assessment · how the grade is built

50% · Mid-term — quiz exam

A 60-question multiple-choice exam on the UMEF LMS, covering Sessions 1–4. It tests the fundamental concepts: the nature of AI projects, planning, resources and risk.

The self-check quizzes in each session here are the best rehearsal — same style, instant explanations.

50% · Final — written assignment

A written exercise, research assignment or case study. You will, for example:

  • Write an in-depth analysis of a topic from the course — e.g. why AI projects fail, or governing model risk.
  • Research one organisation's AI adoption and examine how its projects were managed.
  • Work a practical case study, applying the course concepts to a real situation.

Assessed on critical thinking, research quality and practical application. Your running-project workbook on this site is ideal raw material.
