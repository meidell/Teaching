Brunt · plough design · 1700–1850

The Shape
of the Share

The Industrial Revolution is told in cotton and steam — but one of its quiet engines was the plough. Redesign the share and mouldboard and the same field needs half the draft, half the team. This paper makes mechanical innovation measurable. Pick a plough and feel the difference.

After Brunt, “Mechanical Innovation in the Industrial Revolution: the Case of Plough Design,” Economic History Review 56, 2003. Draft figures are illustrative, scaled to the paper’s account of falling draught force.
